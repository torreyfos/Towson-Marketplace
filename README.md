Our goal with "Towson Marketplace" is to create a digital space where Towson students can sell and offer items to each other. These items may otherwise require students to travel off campus to purchase or pay higher prices at retail stores. Due to Towson Marketplace being campus restricted, it creates a safe and trusted environment that other marketplace options cannot guarantee. It is also a way for students to efficiently use their money such that it won’t interrupt their personal and academic schedules.
